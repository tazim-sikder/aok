Volunteering

Every contribution makes a difference

Volunteers play an essential role in the work of Army of Kindness. The volunteers we get help sort, count, and package all the goods ready for shipment or pickup. There is a job for everyone! By volunteering, you also have an opportunity to make new friends and connections. This is not just an charity. It's a movement everyone can participate in.
